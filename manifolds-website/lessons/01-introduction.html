Lesson 1: What is a Manifold?

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the intuitive idea of a manifold
  • Recognize common examples of manifolds
  • Grasp the concept of "local Euclidean" property

The Intuition

A manifold is a space that locally looks like ordinary Euclidean space, but may have interesting global structure.

Classic Example: The Earth

The surface of the Earth is a 2-dimensional manifold:
